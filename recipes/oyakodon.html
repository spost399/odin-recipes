Oyakodon Recipe


Description

Oyakodon is a traditional Japanese recipe that is simple to make and delicious to taste. The name of the dish literally translates to "Mother and Child Bowl", which is a cheeky reference to the two main ingredients: chicken and eggs. Oyakodon is considered to be Japanese comfort food, and it is a favorite among children and adults.

Oyakodon belongs to a group of Japanese recipes called "donburi", which are basically rice bowls with various toppings. Other common donburi include gyuudon, a sweet and savory beef dish, and katsudon, which comes with a fried pork cutlet.
